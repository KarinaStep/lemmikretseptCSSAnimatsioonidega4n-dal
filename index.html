Pancakes

Ingredient Amount
Eggs 4 pieces
Sugar 60 grams
Wheat flour 200 grams
Salt 1 pinch
Milk 5 dl
Melted butter 50 grams
  1. step - Beat the eggs with sugar, add flour, milk and a pinch of salt and mix until smooth.
  2. step - Finally, add the melted butter and mix.
  3. step - Heat a large pan, add the appropriate amount of batter and cook for a few minutes on medium heat until the pancake is nicely golden on the bottom.
  4. step - Turn around and cook on the other side as well.
